The importance of education


Now our society is making great strides, making education go ahead as well, and of course, the more advanced the education flows to the more successful it is. Because education is part of the enhancement of our lives. Therefore, education is essential in an increasingly modern world. Knowledge is absolutely essential and fundamental to the development of a quality living society. Education helps to build people to be knowledgeable, competent, resourceful, solving problems encountered in everyday life or working in any organization. What we get from education, in addition to being used in our daily life or in our work, we can also apply that knowledge to develop the nation, making our country more prosperous.
