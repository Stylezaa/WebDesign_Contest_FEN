A new modern education


In a world facing a crisis, learning and technology are advancing, it is an alternative to using technology in teaching and learning to develop the skills needed to lift ourselves out of poverty. The growth of technology and the increase in the number of users of online learning services increases learning opportunities anytime, anywhere. And an intensive study review should build and support fundamental skills such as reading, writing, and numeracy, as well as problem-solving, creativity, and the ability to express opinions to others and contribute to Community development Sensitive children and adolescents connect to and access online learning that almost all children have, even those with disabilities who have access to general education. In the future, the World Bank estimates losses of about $ 10 trillion if the learning crisis is not resolved urgently. Therefore, the public and private sectors are requested to cooperate and help each other in conducting this new study.
